About this data
Methodology, scope and benchmarks
▼
What is included: Raw material extraction, processing, dyeing, manufacturing, and distribution to point of sale (cradle-to-gate).

What is not included: Washing, drying, and ironing during the use phase, and end-of-life disposal. Use-phase impacts can exceed production-phase impacts over a garment's lifetime.

Sources: Carbon from Higg Materials Sustainability Index. Water from the Water Footprint Network. Benchmark baseline is a conventional cotton t-shirt.
